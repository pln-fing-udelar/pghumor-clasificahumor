INFORMATION AND CONSENT FORM

This study was certified according to the Informatics Research Ethics Process, RT number 2019/68132 Please take time to read the following information carefully. You should save this page for your records.

Who are the researchers?

The researchers are Julie-Anne Meaney, PhD Candidate under the supervision of Dr Walid Magdy.

What is the purpose of the study?

The purpose of this annotation process is to examine how different people respond to different types of humorous, offensive, and non-humorous texts.

Why have I been asked to take part?

This research targets people of your age group, gender, and level of education.

There are no significant benefits to taking part, but you may find some of the texts funny.

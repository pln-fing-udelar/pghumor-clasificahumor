Instructions

TASK INSTRUCTIONS

In this task, you will imagine that you are a social media moderator. This means that you get to decide what content is published on the social media platform. You make the rules, and they fit in perfectly with your tastes and opinions!

You will see a selection of short texts and you will be asked some questions about the text.

1. Read the text in the box

2. You’ll be asked if the intention of the tweet is to be humorous. By this we mean, is it supposed to have a funny or comic effect, as opposed to a serious effect? This helps us to identify if the genre is humor. You may not personally find the text funny, but you should decide if you think that the intention of the author was to be funny.

3. For the humorous texts, you’ll give your subjective impression of the text - how funny do you find it, on a scale from 1-5? If you identified that the genre is humorous, but you don’t personally find it funny, just choose the lowest rating.

4. After this, you’ll be asked if this text is generally offensive. By this we mean, do you think a reasonable number of people would be offended by this? Do you think it insults another person or group simply for belonging to that group, or do you think it’s inappropriate? If you select yes, we’ll ask you to decide how generally offensive it is, on a scale from 1-5. It does not have to be personally offensive to you, but if you think it is inappropriate, you should select this label. Again, if you select yes, you’ll be asked to select how generally offensive it is, from 1-5.

5. Finally, you’ll be asked if this text is personally offensive. By this we mean, does this target you or a group you belong to, do you feel hurt by this joke, or do you feel hurt on another person’s behalf? If you select yes, you will also be asked to rate this 1-5.

IF YOU FORGET THE DEFINITIONS OF HUMOROUS, GENERALLY OFFENSIVE OR PERSONALLY OFFENSIVE, JUST ROLL YOUR MOUSE OVER THE QUESTION TO GET A REMINDER
